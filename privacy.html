Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2025

Welcome to StickerHunt! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our online quiz game (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Information You Provide via Google Sign-In: When you sign in using Google, we receive information from Google such as your Google ID, email address, and basic profile information associated with your Google account. We use this primarily for authentication and identification.
  • Nickname: You may provide a nickname, or one may be generated for you. This nickname is used for display purposes within the Service, such as on leaderboards.
  • Game Data: We collect information related to your gameplay, including your scores, the difficulty levels you play, and timestamps associated with your scores.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you access and use the Service, potentially including your IP address and approximate location (country code)]. This helps us understand usage patterns and improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Authenticating you and providing access to the Service.
  • Displaying your nickname and scores on leaderboards and within the game interface.
  • Operating and maintaining the Service.
  • Understanding how users interact with the Service to improve it.
  • Personalizing your experience (e.g., showing your rank).
  • Communicating with you, if necessary (e.g., for support requests).

3. Data Storage and Security

Your information (Google ID, nickname, scores, etc.) is stored using Supabase. We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

Google Sign-In: We use Google Sign-In for authentication. Your use of Google Sign-In is subject to Google's Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic. Google Analytics may collect information about your device and Browse activity. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.

Vercel: Our Service is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect technical data. Refer to Vercel's Privacy Policy (https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).

5. Cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies necessary for the functioning of the Service, such as session management via Supabase authentication. We may also use cookies related to Google Analytics.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access or correct your data. Currently, you can update your nickname directly within the Service. For other requests, please contact us.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
