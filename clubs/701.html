Football Club de Nantes, commonly referred to as FC Nantes or simply Nantes (French: [nɑ̃t] ; Breton: Naoned[ˈnãwnət]; Gallo: Naunnt), is a French professional football club based in Nantes in Pays de la Loire. The club was founded on 21 April 1943, during World War II, through a merger of five smaller local clubs based in the city.
